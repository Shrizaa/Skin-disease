Disease:Acne

Acne Symptoms
- *Pimples, blackheads, whiteheads*: Usually on the face, chest, and back.
- *Red, inflamed bumps*: Painful cysts or nodules in severe cases.
- *Scarring*: Can result from persistent or untreated acne.

 Treatment
- *Topical treatments*: Benzoyl peroxide, salicylic acid, or retinoids.
- *Oral medications*: Antibiotics or hormonal treatments (prescribed by a doctor).
- *Gentle skincare*: Non-comedogenic products and regular cleansing.
- *Avoid picking* to prevent scarring.

For severe acne, consult a dermatologist.
