**Melasma** is a skin condition causing brown or gray-brown patches, typically on the face. It's common in women and often triggered by sun exposure, hormonal changes, and genetics.

### Symptoms
- **Patches**: Brown or gray-brown, usually symmetrical, on cheeks, forehead, and upper lip.

### Treatment
1. **Topical Creams**: Hydroquinone, azelaic acid, or kojic acid.
2. **Chemical Peels**: Professional treatments for pigmentation.
3. **Laser Therapy**: For persistent cases.
4. **Sun Protection**: Essential for prevention with SPF 30+.

Consult a dermatologist for the best treatment plan.
